About Me

Born and raised on Long Island, I have always enjoyed creatively solving problems, which is my favorite part of any project. Being a full stack developer allows me to fully engage in this process. I never accept "well that's just the way it's always been done" as an answer. Whether documenting a project or completely refactoring how it's done, I believe that there is always a better, faster, or easier way to do anything.

I enjoy both the technical and personal aspects of projects, the front and back end, the details and the bigger picture, the peanut butter and the jelly.

I appreciate the parallel between people and computers, in that knowing what one wants to communicate or accomplish is as important as conveying it in a way that the person or computer understands. Furthermore, when communication is clear and open, collaboration can flourish.

I like to collaborate throughout all my creative endeavors, whether confronting a mortal enemy / soulmate / a little of both (opera plots are complicated) front and center, or helping out a fellow chorister with notes and rhythms in rehearsal. The best best results stem from everyone can contributing their unique talents.

When not coding, I am singing opera, playing hockey, trading favorite comedy quotes with my friends, or petting the nearest dog. In addition to English I am comfortable in Spanish, Italian, and German.
